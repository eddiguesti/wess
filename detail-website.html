Why This Matters

Guests Call Because the Website Can't Convert

The revenue leak: When a guest visits the WESS website and cannot complete a booking, they either call the hotel (costing staff time) or book through an OTA (costing you commission). A direct booking engine eliminates both. The guest books on your site, at your rate, with zero commission.

OTA commissions typically run at 15-25% of the booking value. Every booking you shift from Booking.com to your own website is pure margin recovery.
Hotels with effective direct booking engines typically convert 3-5% of website visitors. Without a booking engine, the conversion rate is effectively zero. Every interested visitor becomes a phone call or an OTA booking.
The three local companies quoting for the website won't integrate with your channel manager or Opera. We will, because we are building both systems.
